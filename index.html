About Me

Hello! I'm Endah Nurfebriyanti, M.Si., a Mathematics graduate with a strong passion for Data Analytics, Business Intelligence, and Decision Support Systems. My academic background has equipped me with analytical thinking, statistical reasoning, and quantitative problem-solving skills that I apply to transform data into meaningful insights.

I enjoy building interactive dashboards, analyzing business data, and creating data-driven solutions using Python, SQL, Microsoft Excel, Google Looker Studio, and IBM SPSS Statistics. My portfolio showcases projects in sales analytics, KPI monitoring, and business intelligence to support informed decision-making.
